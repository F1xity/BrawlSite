Willow

Willow is a character from the Brawl game series, known for her agility and unique abilities.

Abilities

  • Agility: Willow can move quickly across the battlefield.
  • Special Move: Willow has a special move that allows her to evade attacks.
  • Team Play: She excels in team-based strategies.

Strategy

To play effectively as Willow, focus on mobility and positioning. Use her agility to dodge attacks and strike when the opportunity arises.
